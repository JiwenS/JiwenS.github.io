# Product-Minded Analyst Brand Direction

Version: v1.0.0
Last Updated: 2026-05-14
Owner: Human project owner
Status: Approved

## Context

The owner works in product and development within the finance industry. The owner also follows technology frontiers closely because of personal investment habits.

This background supports a brand direction that combines finance industry practice, product judgment, technical understanding, and market signal awareness.

## Options Considered

- A pure finance analyst positioning.
- A fintech strategy notebook positioning.
- A product-minded analyst positioning with a signal-driven content structure.
- A broad personal media positioning around finance, technology, and product.

## Decision

Adopt the Product-Minded Analyst direction as the core brand position.

Use the Signals / Systems / Products structure from the more media-like concept as the content and navigation layer.

## Rationale

The Product-Minded Analyst direction fits the owner's real professional background in financial products and development work. It also leaves room for technology frontier analysis informed by the owner's investing habits.

The Signals / Systems / Products structure gives the brand a clearer editorial shape without making the positioning too abstract.

## Impact

- Branding should present the owner as a practitioner who uses product thinking to interpret finance and technology.
- Content strategy should include market signals, technical systems, and product analysis as recurring lenses.
- Homepage copy should make the product-minded analyst identity immediately visible.
- Visual direction should feel analytical, current, and high-signal rather than casual or lifestyle-oriented.
