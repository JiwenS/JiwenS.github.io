# English Channel and Personal IP Positioning

Version: v1.0.0
Last Updated: 2026-05-14
Owner: Human project owner
Status: Approved

## Context

The blog is part of the owner's personal IP building process. It should not be only a content archive or personal notebook. It should help the owner express industry understanding and gradually build public influence.

The owner also confirmed that the channel should be English-facing.

## Options Considered

- Chinese-first personal blog focused on local-language distribution.
- Bilingual blog with both Chinese and English content.
- English-first channel for broader professional positioning and international reach.

## Decision

The blog will be an English-first personal channel.

The positioning will combine finance, technology, and product thinking, with emphasis on the owner's industry understanding and personal point of view.

## Rationale

An English-first channel gives the project a broader professional surface area and makes the personal brand easier to align with global finance, technology, product, and AI conversations.

The personal IP goal requires the site to communicate a clear point of view, not only publish isolated articles.

## Impact

- Product positioning documents should define the audience, content pillars, and influence goals in English-first terms.
- Branding documents should define an English voice, tone, and author identity.
- UI/UX should support a professional English reading experience.
- Future content strategy should prioritize clarity, credibility, and point-of-view consistency.
